# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis – Insights & Recommendations

## 🔍 Insight Section

* The dataset includes **5,630 customers**, providing a substantial sample size for analysis.
* The overall **churn rate is 16.84%**, indicating significant customer attrition.
* Customers who prefer logging in with a **computer have slightly higher churn rates** compared to phone users.
* **Tier 1 cities** have lower churn rates than Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.
* **Proximity to warehouse** impacts churn — closer customers show lower churn.
* **Payment behavior insights**:

  * Higher churn: *Cash on Delivery, E-wallet*
  * Lower churn: *Credit Card, Debit Card*
* **Longer tenure → Lower churn**, highlighting importance of early retention.
* Male customers have **slightly higher churn**, but difference is minimal.
* **App usage time** does not significantly impact churn.
* More registered devices correlate with **higher churn**.
* **Order category insights**:

  * Highest churn: *Mobile Phone*
  * Lowest churn: *Grocery*
* Highly satisfied customers (rating 5) still show **unexpected churn**, indicating deeper issues.
* **Single customers** have highest churn, while married customers have lowest.
* Churned customers have **~4 addresses on average**, indicating higher mobility.
* **Customer complaints strongly linked to churn**.
* **Coupon usage is higher among retained customers**, showing effectiveness of incentives.
* Churned customers show **recent disengagement** (short time since last order).
* **Cashback insights**:

  * Moderate cashback → higher churn
  * Higher cashback → lower churn

---

## 🚀 Recommendations to Reduce Churn

* Improve experience for **computer login users** by identifying usability issues.
* Design **city-tier-specific retention strategies**.
* Optimize **logistics & delivery experience**, especially for distant customers.
* Improve **payment experience**:

  * Simplify COD & E-wallet
  * Promote Credit/Debit cards with incentives
* Strengthen **customer support & complaint resolution**.
* Create **targeted offers for high-churn categories** (e.g., Mobile Phones).
* Ensure **consistent experience across devices** (cross-device sync, personalization).
* Proactively engage even **highly satisfied customers**.
* Implement **loyalty programs & personalized offers**.
* Increase **cashback incentives** strategically (use A/B testing).

---

## 📈 Business Impact

By implementing these strategies, the company can:

* Improve customer retention
* Reduce churn rate
* Increase customer lifetime value
* Achieve sustainable long-term growth
